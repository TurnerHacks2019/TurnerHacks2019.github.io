TURNERHACKS 2019

TurnerHacks is a hackathon for high-school students to compete, learn, and explore technology!

WHAT IS TURNERHACKS?

TurnerHacks is a 12 hour hackathon organized by students - for students! Hackers will get the chance to explore new and emerging technologies, participate in interesting workshops, and meet many people. There will also be prizes offered for the winning teams. The day is full of food, fun, tech, friends, and amazing speakers.

Join us on Sunday, June 2nd at Brampton City Hall (Tentative Location) to learn more about the amazing world of tech around us!

FAQs

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event involving collaborative computer programming by including participants at all levels of expertise. In a short period of time, participants attend workshops and work with teammates and mentors to develop some sort of software product. The goal is not to finish, but to leave with a greater understanding and appreciation for computer technology.

When and where will TurnerHacks take place?

TurnerHacks is tentatively scheduled for June 2nd at Brampton City Hall (2 Wellington St W, Brampton, ON L6Y4R2), from 8 AM to 10 PM.

How can I participate in TurnerHacks?

You can apply to be a hacker through our website once hacker signups drop. To be a volunteer, stay updated with out social media pages for applications!

What can I hope to gain from this conference?

Through TurnerHacks, we hope to educate you about the riveting aspects of the cyber world through engaging activities. As our world innovates and enhances our current technologies, basic knowledge in such technologies can be a valuable asset for you to have. We hope to give you an insight on these technologies, and help you develop a foundation of understanding for you to build upon.
